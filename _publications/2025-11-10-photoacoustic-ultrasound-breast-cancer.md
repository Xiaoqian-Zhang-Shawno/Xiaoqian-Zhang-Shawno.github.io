This review surveys the principles, technical progress, and clinical applications of **photoacoustic–ultrasound fusion imaging** in breast cancer diagnosis and therapy. It highlights how multimodal imaging, radiomics, and AI can improve diagnostic specificity, subtype prediction, treatment monitoring, and the assessment of axillary lymph-node metastasis risk.

I see this work as part of a broader effort to connect multimodal sensing with clinically actionable AI systems in medical imaging.
