This review surveys the principles, technical progress, and clinical applications of **photoacoustic-ultrasound fusion imaging** in breast cancer diagnosis and therapy. It highlights how multimodal imaging, radiomics, and AI can improve diagnostic specificity, subtype prediction, and treatment monitoring.

I see this work as part of a broader effort to connect multimodal sensing with clinically actionable AI systems in medical imaging.
