This review article discusses the intersection between **multimodal foundation models** and **ultrasound imaging**, with attention to cross-disciplinary applications, model capabilities, and emerging opportunities in medical AI.

The paper reflects my broader interest in how multimodal models can move from general-purpose representation learning toward task-specific value in clinical imaging workflows.
