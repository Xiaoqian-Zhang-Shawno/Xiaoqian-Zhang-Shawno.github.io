**VQPET** addresses PET image synthesis with a two-stage framework that learns a vector-quantized codebook prior from high-quality PET data and then uses that prior to guide downstream synthesis. The paper introduces a pretrained VQGAN-based prior and a codebook-prior-guided network to improve realism and robustness. The final article appears in volume 45, issue 5 of *IEEE Transactions on Medical Imaging*, pages 2542–2560.

This work aligns with my interest in building stronger representation priors for challenging medical imaging problems.
