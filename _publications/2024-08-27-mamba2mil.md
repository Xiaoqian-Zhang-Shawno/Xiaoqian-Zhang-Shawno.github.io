Computational pathology has rapidly advanced in recent years, while classical multiple instance learning (MIL) methods still face challenges in long-sequence modeling and information utilization on whole slide images (WSIs).

In this work, we propose **Mamba2MIL**, a state space duality based MIL framework that improves long-range dependency modeling for pathology patch sequences. We also design a sequence transformation strategy to better preserve local structure under variable WSI sizes.

Experiments on multiple benchmarks show that Mamba2MIL achieves strong and stable performance for WSI classification tasks.
