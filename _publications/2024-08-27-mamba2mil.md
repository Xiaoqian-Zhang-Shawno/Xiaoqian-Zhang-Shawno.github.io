**Mamba2MIL** studies multiple instance learning for computational pathology with an emphasis on modeling long patch sequences from whole-slide images. The work combines state space duality with weighted feature selection and sequence transformation to build more expressive slide-level representations.

This project reflects my research interest in multimodal and representation-learning methods for medical imaging, especially computational pathology.
